Process
How It Works

Four simple steps from soil sample to crop decision.

01

Enter Soil Data

Input your N, P, K values, temperature, humidity, pH, and rainfall using sliders or direct entry.

02

8 Models Predict

All 8 classifiers run simultaneously — each making an independent crop prediction based on trained patterns.

03

Majority Vote

The crop receiving the most model votes wins. Ties are broken by the highest-accuracy model (Gradient Boost).

04

Actionable Guidance

Receive crop name, soil health score, fertilizer recommendations, and growing tips — all in one dashboard.
